# KETOGENIC HEALTH APP - RESEARCH FRAMEWORK

## Project Overview
Comprehensive research for a Flutter-based ketogenic diet application targeting:
- Weight management
- Metabolic health
- Cardiovascular optimization
- Adjunctive cancer therapy support

## Research Standards
- **Evidence-based**: Prioritize peer-reviewed sources, meta-analyses, RCTs
- **Actionable**: Translate findings into specific app features
- **Current**: Focus on 2023-2025 research where available
- **Nuanced**: Acknowledge limitations, contraindications, and controversies
